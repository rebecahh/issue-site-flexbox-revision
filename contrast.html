Contrast

While some colors like pastels may be aesthetically pleasing, some combinations may be hard to read when overlayed. This is important to keep in mind when designing digital spaces to be more accessible.

Within graphic design, visual accessibility is a key element that determines overall inclusivity. Colour plays an important part in a design, invoking emotions, feelings, and ideas. Colours can also help strengthen a brand's message and perception. (University of Greenwich "Creating Inclusive Content")

Tools

Enter a foreground and background color in RGB hexadecimal format (e.g., #FD3 or #F7DA39) or choose a color using the color picker. The Lightness slider can be used to adjust the selected color. Returns a Contrast Ratio - a ratio of 4.5 : 1 is required for normal text.
